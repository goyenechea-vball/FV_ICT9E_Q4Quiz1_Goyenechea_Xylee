Calculate your BMI

The Body Mass Index is a medical tool that divides your weight in kilograms(kg) divided by the square of your height in meters (m2) to estimate the amount of body fat you have.

How to

Input your current weight in kilograms(kg) on the first text box. Then, input your current height in meters(m) on the second text box. Once done, click the calculate button. (note: no need to input "kg" or "m".
